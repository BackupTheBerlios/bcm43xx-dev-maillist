> 
> IgorsGentooOnNetwork igor # ifconfig eth1 up
> SIOCSIFFLAGS: Protocol error
> 
>

> bcm43xx: PHY connected

> ctrl_interface=/var/run/wpa_supplicant

>   psk="abcdef"

> }
