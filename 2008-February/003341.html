The above error indicates that your copy of 2.6.24 has not been patched to handle the BCM94311 rev
2. This device will not be supported by mainline kernels until 2.6.25-rc1. You should apply the
patch at http://linuxwireless.org/download/b43/patch_2.6.24_for_4311_2.
