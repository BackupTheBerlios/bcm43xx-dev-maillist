Which BCM43XX do you have? You need to open a terminal (in Linux),
issue the command '/sbin/lspci -nnv', and post the first 2 lines for
the section that describes your Broadcom device. If your device is
supported, the driver will be loaded automatically. That is the reason
the guide doesn't tell you to load a driver.
