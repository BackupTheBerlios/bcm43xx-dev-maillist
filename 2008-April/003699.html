The rev 02 BCM4306 cards that use b43legacy had much more of their 
initialization done from the host and less done by the hardware (or by the 
firmware). As a result, the performance is much more dependent on the specs 
as determined by the RE team. In particular, OFDM rates are quite low. I 
plan to revisit this code from the Broadcom drivers to see if it can be 
improved.

As an intermediate test, does bcm43xx work better than b43legacy? If so, I 
will check what might have changed.
