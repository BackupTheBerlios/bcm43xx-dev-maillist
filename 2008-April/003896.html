Pavel Roskin wrote:
> Hello!
> 
> It looks like all tests with the experimental LO calibration code were  
> for bcm4306 and bcm4318.  So I downgraded my Dell laptop from bcm4328  
> to bcm4311 (PCIe Mini Card), so that I can cover this gap and finally  
> switch to a free driver.

I added that patch to my 4311/2 running with wireless-testing on a 
network that uses WPA-PSK TKIP encryption.

Without the patch, a flood ping at 54 Mbs to my AP had a packet loss 
of 0.20%. I could see a burst of losses every 2 minutes when the 
recalibration happened. After applying the patch, the loss rate was a 
factor of 4 smaller at 0.05% and the lost packets were randomly 
distributed.

The patch looks good - ACK from me.
