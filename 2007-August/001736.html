That's why I suggested the lightweight tree:

Makefile
drivers/
  ssb/
    ssb sources
  net/
    wireless/
      bcm43xx_mac80211/
        bcm43xx_mac80211 sources

The top-level Makefile finds the kernel sources and compiles against
them.  It can also have maintenance targets, such as "indent".
